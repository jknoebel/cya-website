Cathedral Young Adults

Our mission is to call young adults to discipleship with Jesus Christ, to sanctify them through formation in our community groups, and to send them out as disciples of Christ as we walk alongside them in Spiritual Accompaniment.

Our Vision

We want to be a community where young adults in the city of Austin and particularly at the parish of St. Mary’s Cathedral: 

  • encounter the Lord in the sacraments, interior prayer and the community
  • choose to accept the invitation of Jesus Christ to be Intentional Disciples
  • strive to become holy through participation in the sacraments, prayer, community, formation
  • go out in service to the parish, the community, the world
  • continue to call, encourage, and teach others to do the same.

Who We Welcome

While the group is primarily made up of single, professional Catholics in their 20s and 30s and events are geared toward this demographic, anyone between the ages of 18-40 is welcome to join, including young married couples.  Our members cover the spectrum in their perspectives on the faith, but whether you are a devout Catholic or haven't been to church in years, you'll find our group is welcoming and nonjudgmental.
